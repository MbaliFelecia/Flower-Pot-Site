Our story

Izimbali means "flowers". I handcraft planters and decor that honour nature's textures and forms. Every vessel is shaped slowly and intentionally, embracing the small variations that make each piece one of a kind.

I work in small batches from my home in Pretoria. Custom orders are welcome—sizes, finishes and sets can be tailored for your space.

Materials & finishes

  • Cement
  • Fabric
  • Sand

Meet the Maker

Hi, I’m Mbali — the hands and heart behind Izimbali. My journey started with a love for nature’s textures and a passion for creating something lasting and beautiful. Each pot I make is a reflection of my dedication to craftsmanship, sustainability, and adding warmth to every space. Whether it’s for your home, garden, or a special gift, I believe in creating pieces that tell a story and bring joy.
